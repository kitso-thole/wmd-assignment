Our History

Founded in 2012, Thole Properties began with a vision to redefine real estate in Botswana.

Established by a team of passionate local experts, Thole Properties started as a small agency in Gaborone, focusing on luxury homes in Phakalane and the CBD. Over the past 12 years, we’ve grown into Botswana’s premier real estate firm, expanding our portfolio to include safari lodges in Maun, riverside retreats in Kasane, and urban apartments in Francistown. Our commitment to quality and client satisfaction has earned us a reputation as the trusted choice for discerning buyers and investors.
